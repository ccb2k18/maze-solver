Maze Solver!

Mode: wall select/delete

  1. Click on a tile to toggle wall on (black) or wall off (blue)
  2. Press the S key and then select the starting tile (green) with your mouse
  3. Press the E key and then select the ending tile (orange) with your mouse
  4. Press the escape key to return to the normal mode of creating/deleting walls
  5. Press enter to have the computer solve the maze (path of solution will be white)!
  6. Press the C key to clear all tiles
